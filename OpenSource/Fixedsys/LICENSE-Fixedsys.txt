The Fixedsys Excelsior font is a "pixelated" style font designed to
emulaetd bitmap computer terminal  fonts. Fixedsys Excelsior was 
designed by Darien Valentine, ligatures have been added to the font
by Kirill Pertsev.  

The font is available freely for both personal and commercial use
under the Creative Commons Zero Dedication license
(https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/deed.en).
